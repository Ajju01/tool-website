What is a Amazon Affiliate Link?

Amazon affiliate program is a popular affiliate commission program on internet. Amazon gives commission to its affiliate for referring users. Many affiliates refer user to a amazon product they like. This attracts many buyers to amazon and in reward amazon offers commisions. It is a win-win for both amazon and its affiliate.
